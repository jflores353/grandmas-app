Merejilda

In Loving Memory
April 2, 1924 - October 18, 2020

Legacy

Merejilda Flores was called by our Heavenly Father to join her biological parents Juanita and Mercedes Rosales, and her adopted parents Josefa Ramos and Juan Gonzales. She will be joining her husband Roman Flores, her three daughters Anna, Carmelita, Paula, and all of her siblings. She is survived by a daughter, six sons, and 132 grandchildren.

Our Mom

There are many women in the world but only one mom gave us life and love. Some mothers come into our lives and quickly go. Some mothers like our mom become friends and stay awhile, leaving footprints on our hearts. Thank you for all you have done for us, and for the footprints you left in our heart.

You always thought of us first and we will forever be grateful. Mom, you will continue to blossom on this earth because of all of the branches from your tree. While some of us cry others will smile because you now join your parents, family, and the Lord who await your arrival. Your love and memory will always be cherished. We're grateful that life picked you for us. We will miss the kind, gentle heart, and warmth you extended to everyone.

Goodbye for now

Before we go our separate ways, let us take leave of our Mom, Merejilda. May our farewell express our affection for her. May it ease our sadness and strengthen our hope. One day we shall joyfully greet her again.
